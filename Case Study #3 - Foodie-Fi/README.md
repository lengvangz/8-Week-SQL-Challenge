Danny wanted to tap into the subscription-based business.  He aimed to create a new streaming services with food-related content - like Netflix, but only cooking shows!

Danny and his friends launched a new startup called Food-Fi, offering customers unlimited, on-demand access to exclusive food content from all over the world. The service is available through monthly or annual subscriptions.
